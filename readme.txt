Ni WooCommerce Bulk Product Editor lets you easily update product details, stock, and prices in bulk for WooCommerce stores.

== Description ==
<p>
Ni WooCommerce Product Editor plugin lists all products in edit mode and lets you change the product name, SKU, price, quantity, and other product information easily.
</p>
<p>
<strong>Ni WooCommerce Product Editor</strong> help you to manage the WooCommerce product price, product stock, product status, product Quantity, product SKU and many other product fields.
</p>
<p>
Show all product type in separate tab, first tab show simple product, second tab variable product and third tab show variation product. Product information are display in tabular format and it’s easily updateable by clicking on update button.
</p>
<p>
Additionally provide the option to filter the product on product name, provide the Ajax pagination and Ajax update for better user experience.
</p>

<h3>Key feature</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Separate product lists for each product type: simple, variation, and variable.</li>
	<li>Easily update product stock.</li>
	<li>Search products by name.</li>
	<li>Manage product inventory.</li>
	<li>Ajax filter, search, and pagination for better user experience.</li>
</ul>
